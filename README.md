# Vetabelle Website

Welcome to the **Vetabelle** website repository! This project is **currently in development** and represents **Vetabelle**, a wellness brand dedicated to empowering women by enhancing their natural beauty and boosting confidence.

The website showcases Vetabelle’s premium supplements designed to promote beauty from within — supporting healthy skin, hair, nails, joints, and muscles through high-quality ingredients like **Collagen**, **Biotin**, and **Keratin**.

## Overview

Vetabelle is a growing wellness brand focused on inspiring women to embrace their inner strength through self-care and beauty from within. The website highlights the following key areas:

- **Shop**: A seamless and secure shopping experience featuring Vetabelle’s wellness products.
- **About Us**: Sharing the brand’s mission, values, and story of empowerment.
- **Customer Experience**: Designed with a modern, elegant aesthetic that’s responsive and easy to navigate across all devices.
- **Education & Wellness**: Encouraging healthy habits through insights on beauty, nutrition, and confidence.
